    2. 1.2) Once all tasks are completed, the user must request a full withdrawal and receive the withdrawal amount before requesting to reset the account.
  2. Withdrawals
    1. 2.1) If the withdrawal amount is 10,000 USDT or more, please contact our online customer service to make a withdrawal.
    2. 1: The maximum withdrawal amount for VIP1 users is 5,000 USDT
    3. 2: The maximum withdrawal amount for VIP2 users is 10,000 USDT
    4. 3: The maximum withdrawal amount for VIP3 users is 20,000 USDT

    6. 2.2) After completing all tasks, you can apply for a full withdrawal.
    7. 2.3) Users need to complete all tasks in order to request a withdrawal.
    8. 2.4) If you choose to give up or withdraw during the task optimization process, you cannot request a withdrawal or refund.
    9. 2.5) Withdrawals cannot be processed if no withdrawal request is received from the user.
  3. Funds
    1. 3.1) All funds will be held securely in the user's account and can be requested in full once all product optimizations are completed.
    2. 3.2) In order to avoid any loss of funds, all data will be processed by the system and not manually
    3. 3.3) The platform will take full responsibility for any accidental loss of funds.
  4. Account Security
    1. (4.1) Please do not disclose your login password and security code to others, and the platform will not be responsible for any loss.
    2. (4.2) Users are not recommended to use their birthday date, ID card number, cell phone number, etc. as the security code or login password.
    3. (4.3) If you forget your login password or security code, please contact our online customer service to reset it.
  5. Normal Products
    1. 5.1) Platform income is divided into normal income and six times income. Each advanced/ordinary user will have 1 to 8 opportunities to obtain six times the profit during the day's optimization tasks. All users typically encounter 1-3 merged data.
    2. 5.2) VIP 1 will receive 0.5% of the profit for each normal optimization product.
    3. 5.3) VIP 1 will get 3% profit from each combined product.
    4. 5.4) Funds and earnings will be credited back to the user's account for each optimization task completed.
    5. 5.5) The system will randomly assign tasks to the user's account based on the total amount in the user's account.
    6. 5.6) Once a task has been assigned to a user's account, it cannot be canceled or skipped.
  6. Combination Tasks
    1. 6.1) Combination product consists of 1-3 products, the user may not necessarily get 3 products, the system will randomly assign the normal goods and combination products, in the combination goods, the user has more chances to get 1-2 products.
    2. 6.2) User will receive 6 times more commissions for each product in the portfolio product than for the regular product.
    3. 6.3) When you receive a Combination Product, all funds will be used for product trade submissions and will be returned to your account balance after you complete the data for each product in the Combination Product
    4. 6.4) The system will randomly allocate combination products based on the total balance of the User's account
    5. 6.5) Once a combination product is assigned to a user's account, it cannot be canceled or skipped.
  7. Deposit
    1. 7.1) The amount of the deposit is chosen by the User. We cannot decide the amount of the deposit for the User and we suggest that the User can make an advance payment according to his ability.
    2. 7.2) If the User needs to pay a deposit when receiving the combined product, we suggest that the User is able to make an advance payment based on the amount of the shortfall shown on the account.
    3. 7.3) Users need to confirm the address with the customer service before depositing, if the user transfers to the wrong address, the platform is not responsible for it
    4. 7.4) The Platform will not be held responsible if the User deposits into the wrong deposit account.

(8.1) The platform has different tasks online and offline every time, if the tasks are not optimized for a long time, the merchant will not be able to unload the tasks, which will affect the progress of the merchant, it is recommended that users complete all tasks and product withdrawals as soon as possible to avoid affecting the progress of the merchant
    2. (8.2) The merchant will provide a deposit detail for the user to deposit.
    3. (8.3) Any delay in completing tasks will be detrimental to the merchant and the process.
  9. Invitation
    1. (9.1) VIP3 users need to work on the platform for 15 working days before they are eligible to invite new users.
    2. (9.2) When you become a VIP4 user and complete the task, you will be eligible to invite other users without any other conditions.
    3. (9.3) It will not be possible to invite other users if the account has not completed all product optimizations
